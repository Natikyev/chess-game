Learn to Play Chess

Five chapters of interactive lessons — from how pieces move to advanced endgame technique. Every rule is shown on a live board.

01
How Pieces Move
Click any piece and see every square it can reach highlighted on a live board. Learn the pawn, knight, bishop, rook, queen and king.
02
Rules & Basics
Castling, en passant, promotion, check, stalemate — every rule illustrated with an interactive board showing exactly what happens.
03
Opening Strategies
The Italian Game, Sicilian Defence, London System and French Defence — with move-by-move board walkthroughs and key principles.
04
Tactics & Patterns
Forks, pins, skewers, discovered attacks and double checks. The tactical patterns every chess player must know, shown on a board.
05
Endgame Essentials
King activity, passed pawns, the opposition, zugzwang and basic checkmates. Win the games you should be winning.
